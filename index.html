About Me

Hi, My name is Christian Chukwu, a curious soul, a builder, and someone who believes that technology should feel like home. I am living in Abidjan, Côte d’Ivoire. I am the owner and manager of Caszmeds Pharmacy, a business I built with a deep sense of purpose: to make healthcare more accessible and compassionate in my community. Every day, I am involved in real conversations, real care, and real impact. At the same time, I am pursuing a degree in Software Development through BYU–Idaho. It’s not just about learning to code it’s about learning to think, solve problems, and create digital experiences that are clean, responsive, and meaningful. I am especially drawn to frontend development, where design meets functionality, and where every detail matters. Thanks for visiting my page. I hope it gives you a glimpse into who I am not just a student or a business owner, but someone who’s building a life with intention.
